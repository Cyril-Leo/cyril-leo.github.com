GameCubePC

The GameCube has become a timeless console, however the technology within has not aged as well. Using the parts from a barebones pc sold by Asrock, the A300 DeskMini, along with a dremel, soldering iron and a 3d printer, I created a working desktop pc hiding in the shell of a GameCube.
